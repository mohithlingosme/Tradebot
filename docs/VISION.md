# Finbot: AI Agentic Trading Bot for Intraday and F&O Trading

## Project Vision

Finbot is an autonomous AI-driven trading agent designed to revolutionize intraday and derivatives trading by leveraging advanced algorithms, real-time data analysis, and risk-managed decision-making. Our vision is to empower traders and investors with a reliable, intelligent system that maximizes returns while minimizing risks in the fast-paced world of financial markets.

## Core Objectives

1. **Autonomous Trading Execution**: Develop an AI agent that can independently analyze market conditions, execute trades, and manage positions without constant human intervention.

2. **Risk-First Approach**: Implement comprehensive risk management protocols to protect capital and ensure sustainable trading performance.

3. **Real-Time Adaptability**: Create a system that can adapt to rapidly changing market conditions through continuous learning and real-time data processing.

4. **Scalable Architecture**: Build a modular, extensible platform that can support multiple asset classes and trading strategies.

5. **Performance Optimization**: Continuously improve trading strategies through backtesting, machine learning, and performance analytics.

## Problems Solved

Finbot addresses key challenges in modern trading:

1. **Human Limitations**: Eliminates emotional decision-making, fatigue, and delayed reactions that often lead to suboptimal trading outcomes.

2. **Information Overload**: Processes vast amounts of market data in real-time to identify opportunities that humans might miss.

3. **Risk Management**: Enforces disciplined risk controls that prevent catastrophic losses from individual trades or market events.

4. **Scalability**: Handles multiple positions and strategies simultaneously, allowing for portfolio diversification and increased market coverage.

5. **24/7 Operation**: Operates continuously during market hours, capitalizing on opportunities across different time zones and market sessions.

## Supported Asset Classes

Finbot is designed to support the following asset classes:

1. **Equities**: Intraday trading of individual stocks and ETFs.
2. **Futures**: Index futures and stock futures for leveraged exposure.
3. **Options**: Call and put options for hedging and speculative strategies.
4. **Currency Derivatives**: Forex futures and options (future expansion).
5. **Commodity Derivatives**: Agricultural and industrial commodity futures (future expansion).

## Risk Limits and Management

Finbot incorporates multiple layers of risk management:

### Position-Level Risk Limits
- **Stop-Loss**: Maximum loss per position (e.g., 1-2% of portfolio value).
- **Take-Profit**: Automatic profit-taking at predefined levels.
- **Position Size**: Maximum exposure per trade (e.g., 5-10% of available capital).

### Portfolio-Level Risk Limits
- **Maximum Drawdown**: Overall portfolio drawdown limit (e.g., 10-15%).
- **Daily Loss Limit**: Maximum loss allowed in a single trading day.
- **Exposure Limits**: Maximum total exposure across all positions.
- **Sector/Asset Concentration**: Limits on exposure to specific sectors or assets.

### Operational Risk Limits
- **Trading Frequency**: Maximum number of trades per day/hour.
- **Liquidity Thresholds**: Minimum liquidity requirements for trade execution.
- **Gap Risk**: Protection against overnight or weekend gaps.

### Systemic Risk Controls
- **Circuit Breaker**: Automatic shutdown during extreme market volatility.
- **Correlation Monitoring**: Tracking of position correlations to avoid concentrated risk.
- **Stress Testing**: Regular simulation of adverse market scenarios.

## Success Metrics
