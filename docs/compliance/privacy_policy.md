# Finbot Privacy Policy

_Last updated: May 12, 2024_

## 1. Introduction
Finbot (“we”, “our”, “us”) provides AI-powered market intelligence for users located primarily in India. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, store, and process personal data.

## 2. Data Collected
- **Account Data** – name, email, phone number, authentication identifiers.
- **Financial Data** – brokerage identifiers, watchlists, user-uploaded datasets.
- **Usage Data** – device metadata, API request metadata, telemetry, diagnostics.

## 3. Legal Basis
Processing is based on user consent, contractual necessity, and compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act.

## 4. Storage & Security
- Data stored in encrypted PostgreSQL (AES-256, pgcrypto).
- Secrets and encryption keys managed via AWS KMS.
- TLS 1.2+ enforced for all transport.

## 5. Data Sharing
We share data only with vetted processors: hosting (AWS), payments (Razorpay), analytics/monitoring (Sentry). No data is sold.

## 6. Retention
PII is retained for the duration of the customer relationship plus 12 months, unless a shorter period is mandated by DPDP.

## 7. User Rights
Users may request access, correction, deletion, or data portability via privacy@finbot.in. Requests fulfilled within 15 business days.
