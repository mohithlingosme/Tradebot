# DPDP Compliance Policy

Finbot adheres to the Digital Personal Data Protection Act (India) through the following controls:

1. **Data Fiduciary Responsibilities**
   - DPO appointed and reachable via dpo@finbot.in.
   - Processing registers maintained in the compliance workspace.
2. **Notices & Consent**
   - Explicit consent obtained for all PII collection points.
   - UI surfaces include concise notices linking to the full Privacy Policy.
3. **Purpose Limitation**
   - Data is processed solely for analytics, personalization, and support.
4. **Childrenâ€™s Data**
   - Finbot does not target users under 18; verification workflows flag suspicious accounts.
5. **Security Safeguards**
   - Encryption in transit and at rest, vulnerability management, quarterly pen tests.
6. **Data Principal Rights**
   - Self-service portal for access/export/delete (backed by ticketing SLA of 15 days).
7. **Breach Notification**
   - Incidents reported to the DPB and affected users within 72 hours.
8. **Cross-Border Transfers**
   - All production data hosted in India (ap-south-1). Limited analytics data processed in the EU with SCCs in place.
