# Finbot Brand Guide

## Visual Identity
- **Primary Colors:** Deep Navy `#020C1B`, Electric Cyan `#1DE9B6`, Amber `#FFB300`.
- **Typography:** Inter for UI, Lexend for headings.
- **Iconography:** Rounded corners, 24px grid, stroke width 1.5px.

## Voice & Tone
Confident, transparent, and compliance-friendly. Avoid sensational trading promises.

## Logo Usage
- Maintain 16px padding.
- Light + dark variants stored in `design/branding/assets/`.
- Never warp or recolor beyond palette.

## Accessibility
Minimum contrast ratio 4.5:1, scalable typography using `rem`, support reduced motion.
