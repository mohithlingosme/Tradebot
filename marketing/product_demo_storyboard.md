# Product Demo Storyboard

1. **Hook (0-10s)**
   - Motion graphic of market volatility + “Meet Finbot.”
2. **Problem (10-25s)**
   - Voice-over describing fragmented tooling, compliance risks.
3. **Solution (25-60s)**
   - Screen capture of dashboard, AI assistant responding with grounded data.
   - Overlay metrics: “<150ms API latency”, “DPDP compliant.”
4. **How It Works (60-90s)**
   - Show ingestion pipeline, AI safety filters, payment flow.
5. **Call to Action (90-105s)**
   - “Sign up for the Finbot Pro beta.”

Asset checklist:
- Updated UI screenshots
- Narration script
- Background music (royalty-free)
- Captions for accessibility
